Privacy Policy & Data Processing

Effective Date: April 2026 | Last Updated: April 3, 2026

1. Introduction & Overview

Welcome to EXNYA. We operate a premium, non-custodial cryptocurrency liquidity and exchange service. Our primary objective is to facilitate the direct buying, selling, and swapping of digital assets for fiat currency (or vice versa) without acting as a custodian for your long-term funds. In delivering this high-speed, direct-settlement infrastructure, the protection of your privacy and the absolute security of your personal data are our highest priorities.

This Comprehensive Privacy Policy ("Policy") outlines exactly how EXNYA ("we," "our," or "us") collects, utilizes, safeguards, and disseminates your personal and financial information when you access our website, utilize our OTC trading desks, or engage with our direct liquidity services.

By registering an account, initiating a trade, or interacting with the EXNYA platform in any capacity, you expressly consent to the data practices meticulously detailed in this document. If you do not agree with any provision set forth herein, you must immediately cease all use of our platform.

2. Information We Collect

To provide a compliant, secure, and rapid exchange environment, we must collect specific categories of information. We adhere to the principle of data minimization, collecting only what is strictly necessary to execute your trades and satisfy global regulatory requirements.

A. Identity Verification (KYC) Data

Because we facilitate the exchange of fiat currency for digital assets, we are legally mandated by Anti-Money Laundering (AML) and Counter-Terrorism Financing (CTF) regulations to identify our clients. We collect:

  • Full legal name, date of birth, and gender.
  • Government-issued identification (e.g., passports, driver's licenses, national ID cards).
  • High-resolution facial biometrics (selfies or live video verification) to match against your provided identification.
  • Proof of residential address (utility bills, bank statements).

B. Financial & Settlement Information

As a non-custodial platform, we rely on external accounts to send and receive your assets. To successfully route your payouts, we collect:

  • Bank account numbers, routing numbers, IBANs, and SWIFT codes for fiat settlements.
  • External cryptocurrency wallet addresses provided by you for the receipt of digital assets.
  • Transaction history, trading volumes, fiat payment histories, and designated cash pickup preferences.

C. Technical & Device Data

We automatically collect technical data to ensure the security of your session and protect against fraudulent activities. This includes your IP address, browser type, operating system, MAC address, session duration, and interactive telemetry data during your use of our platform.

3. How We Use Your Information

The core ethos of EXNYA is privacy and non-custodial freedom. We do not aggregate your data for the purpose of selling it to third-party marketers or advertisers. However, to maintain the integrity of our liquidity networks and ensure your funds arrive safely, data processing is mandatory.

Notice of Data Utilization: While we operate as a direct, non-custodial liquidity provider that does not hold your digital assets, the use of user data may occasionally be needed. We must process, review, and sometimes cross-reference your personal and financial information to successfully route your fiat payouts, verify the legitimacy of your external wallet addresses, and strictly comply with international financial regulations.

Specifically, we utilize your collected data to:

  • Execute Transactions: To process your requests to buy, sell, or swap cryptocurrencies, and to route cash payouts or digital assets to your specified external accounts and wallets.
  • Regulatory Compliance: To perform AML/KYC checks, monitor for suspicious activities, report to financial intelligence units (e.g., FinCEN, FATF), and comply with subpoenas or court orders.
  • Security & Fraud Prevention: To authenticate your identity during login, prevent account takeovers, detect unauthorized transaction patterns, and blacklist malicious IP addresses.
  • Customer Support: To provide white-glove assistance, resolve transaction delays, trace missing fiat wire transfers, and communicate critical updates regarding your account status.

4. How We Share Your Information

EXNYA maintains a strict policy against the monetization of user data. We only share your information with trusted third parties when it is technically necessary to execute your trade or legally required by governing authorities.

A. Banking and Liquidity Partners

To issue your physical cash, domestic ACH, or international wire transfers, we must share your legal name and routing information with our tier-one banking partners and payment processors. These partners are bound by strict confidentiality agreements and banking secrecy laws.

B. KYC/AML Verification Providers

We utilize globally recognized, secure third-party identity verification agencies to validate your government IDs and biometrics. These agencies verify your documents against global watchlists and immediately purge temporary biometric data in accordance with their strict data retention policies.

C. Legal and Regulatory Authorities

If compelled by a valid subpoena, court order, or formal request from a recognized law enforcement agency, we will disclose your information. We reserve the right to report any activity we suspect violates laws or regulations to appropriate authorities without prior notice to you.

5. Data Security Protocols

Because you trust us with sensitive financial routing and identity documents, we employ a multi-layered security architecture designed to thwart unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal data.

  • End-to-End Encryption: All data transmitted between your device and EXNYA's servers is encrypted using AES-256 and TLS 1.3 cryptographic protocols.
  • Offline Data Vaulting: Highly sensitive KYC documents are encrypted and moved to air-gapped cold storage servers, significantly reducing the risk of a centralized database breach.
  • Strict Access Controls: Internal access to user data is heavily restricted. Only dedicated compliance officers with multi-signature authorization can view KYC records, and all access is logged and audited.

Despite our extensive security measures, no system is impenetrable. By using EXNYA, you acknowledge the inherent risks of transmitting information over the global internet.

6. Data Retention

As a regulated financial entity, we cannot immediately delete your data upon request if you have executed trades on our platform. Global AML regulations require us to retain your identity documents, transaction records, and associated external wallet addresses for a minimum period of five (5) to seven (7) years after your account is closed or your last transaction is completed.

Once this legally mandated retention period expires, your data will be permanently and securely eradicated from our servers using cryptographic wiping methods.

7. Your Rights and Controls

Depending on your geographic jurisdiction (such as the GDPR in Europe or the CCPA in California), you possess specific rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to Access: You may request a complete cryptographic export of all personal data and transaction history associated with your EXNYA account.
  • Right to Rectification: You may request corrections to any inaccurate or incomplete financial routing or identity information.
  • Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): You may request the deletion of your account. However, as stated in Section 6, this right is superseded by our legal obligation to retain transaction data for AML purposes.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You may request that we temporarily halt processing your data while a dispute or investigation is ongoing.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact our dedicated Privacy Office via the information provided at the end of this policy.

8. External Wallet Risks & Blockchain Transparency

EXNYA is fundamentally non-custodial. When you purchase cryptocurrency from us, we broadcast the transaction to the public blockchain to deliver the assets to your designated external wallet. Please be acutely aware that blockchains (like Bitcoin and Ethereum) are public, immutable ledgers.

While EXNYA protects your identity internally, the wallet address you provide and the amount of cryptocurrency we send to it will be permanently visible to the public. We cannot control, modify, or erase data once it is confirmed on a decentralized blockchain network. You are solely responsible for maintaining the privacy of your own external wallet addresses.

9. Policy Modifications

The regulatory landscape surrounding digital assets is highly volatile. Consequently, EXNYA reserves the absolute right to amend, update, or entirely rewrite this Privacy Policy at our discretion. Any material changes will be communicated to you via the email address associated with your account and through prominent notifications on our platform. Your continued use of EXNYA after such modifications constitutes your formal acceptance of the revised terms.
