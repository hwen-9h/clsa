How EXNYA Works

We don't hold your funds. We simply facilitate the exchange. Here is how you can buy, sell, or swap in 5 easy steps.

1

Initiate Your Request

Log into your EXNYA account and open a new trade ticket. Tell us exactly what you want to do: Buy crypto with fiat, sell your crypto for a cash payout, or swap one asset for another.

2

Lock in Your Rate

Our system will provide you with a real-time, highly competitive quote based on current market liquidity. Once you confirm, the rate is locked in for the duration of the transaction window.

3

Provide Destination Details

Because we do not use platform wallets, you must tell us where to send your assets. If you are buying crypto, provide your personal wallet address. If you are selling, provide your bank details or arrange for a physical cash pickup.

4

Send the Funds / Assets

Follow the instructions to send your fiat payment to our banking partners, or transfer your cryptocurrency to the secure, one-time EXNYA receiving address generated for your specific trade.

5

Receive Instant Settlement

As soon as we verify the receipt of your funds or the required blockchain confirmations, we immediately dispatch your payout. No holding periods, no withdrawal limits—just direct delivery.

The Ultimate Security: Non-Custodial

The safest exchange is the one that doesn't hold your money.

Zero Custodial Risk

Unlike traditional exchanges that hold your crypto in massive, vulnerable honeypots, EXNYA is purely a settlement layer. You retain complete control of your private keys right up until the moment of exchange. We never hold your assets long-term.

Encrypted Communications

All trade details, bank account information, and wallet addresses are transmitted via AES-256 encryption. Your personal identity and financial routing data are shielded from third parties at all times.

Stringent AML & KYC

While we value your privacy, we operate strictly within the law. All clients undergo rigorous Know Your Customer (KYC) and Anti-Money Laundering (AML) checks to ensure that all fiat and crypto entering our ecosystem is clean, authorized, and safe to transact with.

Wallet Verification

To prevent fraud and accidental loss of funds, our systems utilize advanced algorithmic checks to verify that the external wallet addresses you provide are valid and correspond to the correct blockchain network before we ever send a payout.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you provide a wallet to hold my crypto? +
No. EXNYA is a non-custodial direct exchange. You must have your own personal wallet (like Trust Wallet, Ledger, Trezor, or MetaMask) to receive crypto when you buy from us, or to send crypto from when you are selling to us. This ensures you always remain in control of your funds.
How do I receive my cash when I sell my crypto? +
When initiating a sell order, you will select your preferred payout method. We offer direct bank wire transfers, local ACH/domestic transfers, and in certain jurisdictions, authorized physical cash payouts through our premium OTC desks.
How long does a transaction take? +
Crypto-to-crypto swaps are nearly instantaneous once the blockchain confirms your deposit. For crypto-to-fiat sales, bank transfers are typically dispatched within 15 to 30 minutes of blockchain confirmation, though actual bank crediting times may vary depending on your financial institution.
Is there a minimum or maximum trade limit? +
Because we cater to both retail and institutional clients, our limits are highly flexible. While standard trades have accessible minimums, our dedicated OTC block trading desk is equipped to handle single transactions upwards of $1,000,000+ with deep liquidity and zero market slippage.
